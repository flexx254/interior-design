Our Portfolio

See how we’ve transformed spaces into timeless masterpieces.

Modern Living Room

Status Before

The office space was outdated, cramped, and lacked a cohesive design. Furniture was mismatched and lighting was poor, creating an uninspiring work environment.

Strategy Used

We adopted a minimalist design approach with a focus on functionality, natural lighting, and a muted color palette that complements. Ergonomic furniture, smart lighting, and acoustic treatments were key upgrades.

Final Look

The transformed space now radiates professionalism and calm. Clean lines, soft greens, and warm lighting work together to create a sleek, inviting executive office ideal for high-level productivity.

Executive Office

Status Before

The executive office was cluttered and lacked identity. The color scheme was inconsistent, and the workspace felt disconnected from the company’s vision and leadership role it was meant to reflect.

Strategy Used

We focused on redefining the space through intentional zoning — separating areas for focused work, private meetings, and relaxation. A calm, earthy color palette centered around #A3B18A was introduced, complemented by soft textiles and modern lighting fixtures to improve both comfort and aesthetics.

Final Look

The final result is a cohesive and sophisticated executive office. Subtle green hues bring freshness, while clean furniture lines and strategic lighting create an atmosphere of quiet authority and focus, perfect for decision-making and leadership.

Minimalist Kitchen

Status Before

The office was dark and uninspiring, with dated fixtures and no defined purpose for different work activities. It lacked personal touches and failed to convey the executive’s role or vision.

Strategy Used

Our approach emphasized clarity and calm. We introduced natural materials, opened up the layout, and selected a color scheme led by #A3B18A to reflect balance and authority. Functional zones were created for meetings, deep work, and reflection, using lighting and spatial dividers.

Final Look

The redesigned office now feels open, grounded, and empowering. Soft green tones, elegant furnishings, and refined lighting bring out a sense of purpose and poise — a true reflection of thoughtful leadership.

Elegant Bedroom

Status Before

The space was originally underutilized, with a bland layout and generic furnishings. It lacked character and did little to support focus, comfort, or professional meetings.

Strategy Used

We reimagined the environment with a focus on clarity, comfort, and identity. Using #A3B18A as a guiding color, we layered natural textures, introduced built-in storage, and added lighting that could adapt to different tasks and moods throughout the day.

Final Look

The new office feels personalized and grounded. With soft greens, clean architecture, and thoughtfully placed elements, the space now supports productivity while reflecting the executive’s unique leadership style.

Retail Space

Status Before

The office lacked warmth and intention. Cold surfaces, generic decor, and poor spatial organization made it feel more like a temporary holding area than a leader’s workspace.

Strategy Used

We restructured the space to prioritize flow and function, introducing biophilic elements and a color scheme led by #A3B18A to create a serene, confident atmosphere. We integrated subtle textures and strategic lighting to soften the environment and add depth.

Final Look

The final space is balanced and inspiring — a fusion of nature and professionalism. Every detail, from the soft green accents to the tailored layout, now speaks to calm leadership and intentional design.

Contemporary Dining

Status Before

The original office felt disconnected and lacked a clear visual identity. It was filled with bulky furniture, minimal daylight, and no cohesive color story, which affected both mood and efficiency.

Strategy Used

We began by opening up the space and decluttering. Using #A3B18A as the anchor tone, we introduced complementary natural shades, upgraded the lighting design, and replaced outdated furniture with sleek, modern alternatives that reflected the executive’s personality.

Final Look

The result is a polished and functional executive suite. With soothing greens, smart lighting, and elegant finishes, the office now communicates clarity, calm authority, and modern professionalism.
